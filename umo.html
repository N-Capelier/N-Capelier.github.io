Unity Modules
 



Welcome to the UMO main page !

UMO is a collection of packages for your Unity projects offering you free unity packages with tools and objects to help you save time while working.
UMO is updated constantly. Feels free to suggest any improvement to the existing tools! You can contact me by email at capelier.nicolas@gmail.com.

Packages

[A]ssets, [T]ools and [U]tilities are grouped by packages.

  • [U] Countdown Timer
  • [U] Lerpers
  • [T] Scene Viewer
  • [U] Singleton
  • [T] Sprite Sheet Creator
