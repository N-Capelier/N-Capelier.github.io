Who I am

Hello! My name is Nicolas and I am a junior Technical Game Designer studying at RUBIKA SupinfoGame in France.

I do Game Design

I like to concept new games with unique mechanics and systems, finding inspiration from online multiplayer games and sandbox games to give the player unique ways to have fun. I am also used to work with Agile methods of production as I had the opportunity to use them in short and long time projects.

And programming

I enjoy programming game features and prototyping concepts to get a quick overview of the potential of the systems and mechanics but also identify the challenges in the production of games. I appreciate to work with Unity3D and C# and I am currently learning Unreal Engine's workflow with blueprints. Finally I usually create tools for my projects to facilitate the development of my games.

What am I good at?

Creative Ideas

I can design game systems and mechanics. Create documentation and share my work with everyone in the team. I am used to work with many people specialized in different aspects of game development.

Programming

For the past three years, I have been working with Unity3D and I really enjoy programming. I can create game prototypes but also work on features that need to be tested.

Easy and useful tools

I have never done a project without creating tools. Extending the game engine editor I am working on always allowed the team I am working in to save time and keep their productivity to its maximum potential.

Communication between teams

I can easily communicate with different teams. I can especially produce technical documentation for game programmers to understand game features faster. I can also provide a technical point of view during the conception and anticipate technical difficulties.
